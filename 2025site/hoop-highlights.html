Hoop Highlights

I shoot and edit basketball highlights for two main purposes. For scouting, I capture game footage and create detailed edits that break down player performance, positioning, and decision-making for coaches and scouts evaluating talent. This work requires understanding the game deeply and knowing what details matter most for different positions and levels of play.

I also create highlight reels for Instagram and other social platforms, editing game footage into compelling short-form content that showcases players, teams, and memorable moments. This side of the work balances entertainment value with highlighting real skill and athleticism.

For comprehensive game films, photographs, and highlight reels, check out my @mcwstarzmedia Instagram. For more extensive content including full games, detailed breakdowns, and archived highlights, visit my Patreon for MCW Starz.
