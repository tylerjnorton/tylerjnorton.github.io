Hummingbird

I created the complete brand identity for Hummingbird Nutritious Eats, a restaurant in Massachusetts. This included the logo design, comprehensive branding system, restaurant signage, menus, and their website. The work focused on creating a cohesive visual identity that reflects their focus on fresh, healthy food while maintaining a welcoming and energetic brand presence.

Visit their site at hummingbirdnutritiouseats.com.
