MCW Starz Videos

I've produced mini documentary style videos for MCW Starz AAU, a youth basketball program dedicated to developing young talent. These videos follow individual players and tell the story of their basketball journey from the ground up.

Each piece features the people who matter most in a player's development: coaches who guide them, teammates who push them, parents who support them, and mentors who shape their character both on and off the court. The goal is to capture authentic moments that show what it takes to grow in the game.

This work combines storytelling with sports videography, pulling together interviews, game footage, practice moments, and real life to create something that feels genuine and meaningful.

I'm available for documentary and video commissions. If you're interested in producing similar content, reach out at tyler.j.norton@gmail.com.
